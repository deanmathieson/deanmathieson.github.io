About Us

Toast.House is a full service web design agency based in Edinburgh.

We design and create engaging and dynamic websites which are fully responsive on any mobile device, our aim is to build websites that exceed our clients’ expectations every time.

Our Goals

Our goal is to help businesses and individuals increase their web presence.

We have more than 10 years’ experience designing and developing websites for clients across all sectors, ranging from small boutique and start up companies to Larger organisations.

Let us work with you to create your vision and increase your visibility to the largest possible audience through our specialist knowledge in website design, development and search engine optimisation. Whether that is to increase visibility to attract new customers, to display the services you offer or just give your website a fresh new look. Our aim is to exceed our clients expectations every time and we will not stop until each aspect is perfect.
